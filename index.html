It has been instrumental in providing a plethora of opportunities for students to enhance their careers by pursuing technical internships abroad since its inception in 2005.

What our members say

My IAESTE Internship was priceless , the main benefits came down to three elements. I thank IAESTE for giving me this opportunity which I will embrace all my life.
